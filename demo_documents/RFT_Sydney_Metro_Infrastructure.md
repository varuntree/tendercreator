# REQUEST FOR TENDER (RFT)

## Sydney Metro Western Extension - Infrastructure Package

**RFT Number:** TfNSW-2025-SMWE-001
**Issue Date:** January 15, 2025
**Closing Date:** February 28, 2025, 2:00 PM AEST
**Contract Value:** $45-65 Million AUD
**Contract Period:** 24 months

---

## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Transport for NSW (TfNSW) invites qualified contractors to submit tenders for the design, construction, and commissioning of critical infrastructure works for the Sydney Metro Western Extension project.

### 1.1 Project Scope

The works comprise:

- Construction of 3.2km twin tunnel infrastructure between Westmead and Sydney Olympic Park
- Installation of underground utilities and drainage systems
- Construction of ventilation and emergency access facilities
- Track infrastructure and systems integration
- Environmental management and rehabilitation works

### 1.2 Project Location

Works will be undertaken across multiple sites:
- Primary site: Westmead to Sydney Olympic Park corridor
- Support sites: TBA after contractor appointment
- Affected areas: Parramatta, Auburn, Lidcombe precincts

### 1.3 Project Objectives

- Deliver infrastructure on time and within budget
- Maintain highest safety standards (zero harm)
- Minimize disruption to existing transport services
- Achieve environmental sustainability targets
- Engage local community and stakeholders effectively

## 2. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Tenderers must submit the following documents as part of their tender response:

### 2.1 Technical Specification (Mandatory)
**Requirements:**
- Detailed technical approach to tunnel construction methodology
- Proposed construction sequencing and staging
- Plant and equipment deployment strategy
- Quality control procedures and testing regimes
- Integration approach with existing metro systems
- Geotechnical risk assessment and mitigation strategies
- Utilities management and relocation procedures
- Traffic and access management during construction

**Page Limit:** 40-60 pages
**Reference:** Technical Specification Template - Appendix A

### 2.2 Bill of Quantities (Mandatory)
**Requirements:**
- Complete itemized cost breakdown for all works
- Labour, materials, plant, and equipment costs
- Provisional sums for identified variable items
- Daywork rates and schedules
- Cost allocation per project phase
- Cash flow projection over contract period
- Cost escalation assumptions and allowances
- Breakdown must align with provided BoQ template

**Format:** Excel spreadsheet using provided template
**Reference:** BoQ Template - Appendix B

### 2.3 Project Methodology (Mandatory)
**Requirements:**
- Detailed construction methodology for all major work packages
- Tunnel boring machine (TBM) selection and operation methodology
- Excavation support and ground stabilization techniques
- Concrete batching, delivery, and placement procedures
- Spoil management and disposal strategy
- Site establishment and logistics arrangements
- Interface management with other contractors
- Construction program with critical path analysis

**Page Limit:** 30-50 pages
**Reference:** Section 3.4 - Methodology Requirements

### 2.4 Risk Management Plan (Mandatory)
**Requirements:**
- Comprehensive risk register identifying all project risks
- Risk assessment methodology (AS/NZS ISO 31000:2018)
- Risk mitigation strategies for high-priority risks
- Risk monitoring and review processes
- Contingency planning for critical risks
- Insurance and liability management
- Third-party risk assessment (adjacent properties, utilities)
- Emergency response procedures

**Format:** Risk register + narrative report
**Page Limit:** 20-30 pages

### 2.5 Quality Assurance Plan (Mandatory)
**Requirements:**
- Quality management system overview (ISO 9001 compliant)
- Inspection and test plans (ITPs) for all key activities
- Quality control testing frequencies and methodologies
- Non-conformance management procedures
- Document control and records management
- Subcontractor quality management requirements
- Quality audit schedule and procedures
- Qualification and certification requirements for personnel

**Page Limit:** 25-35 pages
**Standards:** Must comply with ISO 9001:2015

### 2.6 Work Health & Safety Plan (Mandatory)
**Requirements:**
- Safety management system aligned with AS/NZS 4801
- Site-specific safety risk assessments and controls
- Safety training and induction procedures
- Personal protective equipment (PPE) requirements
- Plant and equipment safety management
- Confined spaces and high-risk work procedures
- Emergency management and evacuation plans
- Incident reporting and investigation procedures
- Safety performance monitoring and KPIs
- Contractor and subcontractor safety management

**Page Limit:** 30-40 pages
**Critical:** Must demonstrate zero-harm safety culture

### 2.7 Environmental Management Plan (Mandatory)
**Requirements:**
- Environmental management system (ISO 14001 compliant)
- Environmental impact assessment and mitigation measures
- Soil and water quality management procedures
- Noise and vibration monitoring and control
- Air quality and dust management
- Flora and fauna protection measures
- Waste management and recycling strategy

- Environmental monitoring program

**Page Limit:** 25-35 pages
**Reference:** Environmental Impact Statement - Appendix C

### 2.8 Project Timeline & Schedule (Mandatory)

- Subcontractor management and coordination procedures

### 2.10 Stakeholder Engagement Strategy (Mandatory)

## 3. EVALUATION CRITERIA

Tenders will be evaluated against the following weighted criteria:

| Criterion | Weighting |
|-----------|-----------|
| Technical Capability and Methodology | 30% |
| Cost and Value for Money | 25% |
| Risk Management and Quality Assurance | 15% |
| Safety Performance and WHS Plan | 15% |
| Project Team Experience and Capability | 10% |
| Environmental and Sustainability Approach | 5% |

**Total:** 100%

### 3.1 Minimum Qualification Requirements

Tenderers must demonstrate:
- Minimum $200M annual turnover for last 3 financial years
- Experience delivering at least 2 comparable tunnel infrastructure projects (>$30M value) in last 7 years
- Current ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and AS 4801 certifications
- Prequalification under TfNSW contractor scheme (Category T1 or higher)
- Professional indemnity insurance minimum $20M
- Public liability insurance minimum $50M

## 4. CONTRACT CONDITIONS

### 4.1 Contract Type
AS 4902-2000 General Conditions of Contract (Design and Construct)

### 4.2 Payment Terms
Monthly progress claims based on actual work completed

### 4.3 Liquidated Damages
$50,000 per day for delays beyond practical completion date

### 4.4 Retention
5% of contract value retained until end of defects liability period (12 months)

### 4.5 Performance Security
10% of contract value as bank guarantee or insurance bond
