NSW GOVERNMENT CONSTRUCTION TENDER
PROJECT: Community Centre Redevelopment
Contract Number: CC-2025-091
Issue Date: November 2025

===========================================
SECTION 1: PROJECT OVERVIEW
===========================================

The NSW Government invites tenders for the design, construction, and commissioning of a new community centre facility in Western Sydney. The project includes demolition of the existing structure, construction of a 2,500 sqm multi-purpose facility, landscaping, and parking facilities.

Project Value: $12-15 million
Project Duration: 18 months from contract signing
Site Location: 145 Community Drive, Western Sydney NSW 2000

===========================================
SECTION 2: SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
===========================================

Tenderers must submit the following documents by the deadline:

2.1 Technical Specification
Complete technical approach document describing construction methodology, project timeline, quality assurance processes, and resource allocation plan.

2.2 Bill of Quantities
Itemized cost breakdown for all work packages including materials, labor, equipment, and contingencies. Must align with Australian Standard cost codes.

2.3 Project Plan
Detailed project schedule with milestones, critical path analysis, and resource loading. Must include Gantt charts and dependency mapping.

2.4 Risk Register
Comprehensive risk assessment covering construction, financial, environmental, and stakeholder risks. Must include mitigation strategies and risk owners.

2.5 Work Health & Safety Plan
Complete WHS plan compliant with NSW WHS Act 2011. Must address site safety, emergency procedures, and safety training programs.

2.6 Quality Assurance Plan
Quality management framework describing inspection schedules, testing protocols, and quality control measures throughout project lifecycle.

2.7 Environmental Management Plan
Environmental impact assessment and management strategies addressing noise, dust, waste management, and protection of existing flora.

2.8 Subcontractor List
List of all proposed subcontractors with trade classifications, licensing details, insurance coverage, and past performance records.

2.9 Company Profile
Organization overview including company history, organizational structure, key personnel CVs, financial capacity statements, and past project references.

2.10 Insurance Certificates
Current insurance certificates including Public Liability ($20M), Professional Indemnity ($5M), Workers Compensation, and Contract Works insurance.

SECTION 12: EVALUATION CRITERIA
===========================================

Tenders will be evaluated against the following weighted criteria:

12.1 Price (30%)
- Total tendered price competitiveness
- Value for money assessment
- Payment schedule reasonableness
- Risk and contingency allowances

12.2 Technical Capability (25%)
- Construction methodology and innovation
- Program achievability and realism
- Quality assurance framework
- Risk management approach

12.3 Experience and Track Record (20%)
- Similar project experience
- Key personnel qualifications and experience
- Past performance and client references
- Financial capacity and stability

12.4 WHS Performance (15%)
- WHS management system robustness
- Safety performance statistics (LTIFR)
- Safety culture and commitment
- Training and competency programs

12.5 Environmental and Sustainability (10%)
- Environmental management approach
- Sustainability initiatives and targets
- Waste minimization strategies
- Energy and water efficiency measures
